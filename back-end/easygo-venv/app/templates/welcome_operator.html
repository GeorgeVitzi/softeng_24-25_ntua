EasyGo is an interoperability provider for tolling systems on highways in Greece, with different automatic passage systems!

EasyGo is independent from the highway operators. Our mission is the interoperability of these highways, as well as providing services for data availability and analysis of highway usage data.

Our application collects passage data from operators and stores it accordingly.Then, it periodically calculates the financial settlements between operators and makes data available for analysis to third parties. We also conduct some of these analyses ourselves.

EasyGo also allows users/e-pass holders to reload their target,as well as access various statistical data.
